#ScriptHawk
Extra Lua scripts and RAM watches for the [BizHawk](https://github.com/TASVideos/BizHawk) emulator. Currently focused on DK64 but expanding rapidly to other games.

##Supported Games
###Donkey Kong 64
- Europe
- US
- US (Kiosk)
- Japan

###Banjo Kazooie
- Europe
- US 1.0
- US 1.1
- Japan

###Super Mario 64
- Europe
- US
- Japan
- Japan (Shindou Edition)
